Kolibri Platform – Offline Education That Works

As part of my digital education mission through Mavinstech Solution, I work extensively with Kolibri—a powerful, free, and open-source platform designed to deliver quality learning experiences without the need for internet connectivity. Whether in remote schools, refugee camps, or underserved rural communities, Kolibri is proving that offline education can work effectively.

Why Kolibri?

  • Operates fully offline after initial setup
  • Provides interactive lessons, videos, quizzes, and learner progress tracking
  • Customizable to local curriculums and languages

What I Offer

At Mavinstech Solution, I provide end-to-end Kolibri deployment services tailored to each learning environment. These services include:

  • Installation and configuration of Kolibri on various hardware setups
  • Hands-on training for teachers, staff, and learners
  • Curriculum digitization, focusing on the Compassion International curriculum
  • Ongoing technical support and capacity building

I’m proud to integrate Kolibri into my work, helping ensure that every learner—regardless of location or connectivity—has access to engaging, high-quality education. Being part of the Kolibri global community strengthens my vision to bridge the digital divide and empower sustainable learning in even the most remote areas.

About Kolibri

Kolibri is a free, open-source educational platform designed for offline use in schools, libraries, and community centers. It enables learners to access a rich library of high-quality content, including videos, exercises, and lessons — even without internet access.

  • Ideal for remote areas with limited or no connectivity
  • Includes content from Khan Academy, CK-12, and more
  • Customizable for local curricula and languages
  • Supports self-paced, blended, and group learning

Kolibri helps bridge the digital divide by making learning accessible to everyone, everywhere.
