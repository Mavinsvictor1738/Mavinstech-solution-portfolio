Kolibri Platform – Offline Education That Works

Kolibri is a free, open-source platform designed to make quality educational content accessible without internet. Ideal for remote schools, refugee camps, and underserved communities.

  • Works fully offline after initial setup
  • Includes interactive lessons, videos, quizzes, and tracking tools
  • Customizable to local curriculum and languages
  • Runs on low-cost hardware like Raspberry Pi or old PCs

Mavinstech Solution offers complete Kolibri deployments—installation, teacher training, and content customization to help learners thrive even in low-resource environments.

About Kolibri

Kolibri is a free, open-source educational platform designed for offline use in schools, libraries, and community centers. It enables learners to access a rich library of high-quality content, including videos, exercises, and lessons — even without internet access.

  • Ideal for remote areas with limited or no connectivity
  • Includes content from Khan Academy, CK-12, and more
  • Customizable for local curricula and languages
  • Supports self-paced, blended, and group learning

Kolibri helps bridge the digital divide by making learning accessible to everyone, everywhere.
